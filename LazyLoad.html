About Us

Fix's Dog Daycare is dedicated to providing a safe, fun, and nurturing environment for dogs while their owners are at work or away. Our professional team ensures your pet's happiness and well-being every step of the way.

Our Services

  • Full-day and half-day daycare options
  • Socialization and playgroup activities
  • Basic obedience training
  • Grooming services (bathing, nail trimming, brushing)
  • Pet taxi services
  • Retail sales: Pet toys, treats, and accessories

Pricing

Our pricing is designed to be affordable and transparent:

  • Full-Day Daycare: $30
  • Half-Day Daycare: $20
  • Basic Obedience Training: $50 per session
  • Grooming Services: Starting at $25
